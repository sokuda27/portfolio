From syllabus: This independent study covers the use of data-driven techniques such as the singular value decomposition (SVD), least squares, and dynamic mode decomposition (DMD) - eventually leading to an overview of data-driven control, deep reinforcement learning, and model-order reduction. Applications in image processing, fluid flow control, and robotic manipulation will be discussed.

To study the application of SVD, a dataset on the wake of an airfoil was analyzed. The velocties were reconstructed by inspecting the eigenvalues and modes.
