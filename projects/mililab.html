Because cameras are very sensitive to color, measuring heartbeat using a video feed is possible. The heartbeat monitoring algorithm used minute fluctuations in face redness to determine heart rate, but the calibration time was high and accuracy was low. To combat this, Google's MediaPipe algorithm was implemented to introduce a dynamic region of interest (ROI) that moved with the user. This significantly reduced the calibration time of the algorithm.
